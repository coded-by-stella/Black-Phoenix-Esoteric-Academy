Which Witch Are You?
The 9 Paths to Begin Walking Your Magic

Rise, learn and master your magic.

Not all witches practice in the same way. The 9 introductory paths of the Black Phoenix Esoteric Academy are designed for beginners who feel the call of magic and want to understand where they resonate the most before choosing a deeper, professional path.

These are short, structured journeys that help you understand what kind of witch you are, build solid and safe foundations and grow in awareness, so you can access the Professional Path of the 7 Pillars or practice your magic for yourself in a more grounded way.

How the 9 Paths Work

Each path is designed for complete or almost-complete beginners. It includes theoretical lessons, simple exercises and basic rituals, and is based on the content of the 7 Pillars in a guided and simplified format.

At the end of the path, you will receive a participation certificate dedicated to the type of witch you explored and a continuity map that shows you which Pillars to follow next if you want to enter the professional program of the Black Phoenix Esoteric Academy.

The 9 Introductory Paths

Choose the path that speaks most to your heart, or begin with the ‚ÄúWhich Witch Are You?‚Äù test to discover where to start.

Green Witch Path

Magic of plants, forests and living earth.

For those who feel the call of trees, herbs, resins and moist soil. You learn the basics of green magic, plant allies and small rituals rooted in nature.

Lunar Witch Path

Walking in rhythm with the phases of the moon.

For those who speak with the moon and feel the nights move something within. You learn to follow the lunar phases, create simple rituals and live the month as a sacred cycle.

Elemental Witch Path

Earth, air, fire, water: the circle of the elements.

For those who feel moved by fire, water, air and earth. You learn to know the elements, work with them safely and use them as the structure of your practice.

Hearth Witch Path

The home as a temple, the kitchen as an altar.

For those who feel that home, kitchen and daily habits are already rituals. You learn domestic magic, hearth magic, food magic and the power of everyday objects.

Tarot & Oracle Witch Path

Tarot, oracles and symbolic messages.

For those fascinated by Tarot, oracles and symbols that speak through images. You learn the basics of intuitive reading, respect for the oracles and how to use cards for self-discovery.

Shadow Witch Path

Shadow work, depth and transformation.

For those who feel the call of the deep side, of dreams, wounds and inner transformation. You learn the foundations of shadow work, shadow magic and dialogue with your hidden parts.

Spirit & Ancestral Witch Path

Bloodline, presences and the invisible world.

For those who perceive presences, feel the dead nearby or sense a deep bond with their ancestral line. You learn the basics of working with ancestors and spirits, with a strong focus on safety, boundaries and ethics.

Cosmic Witch Path

Planetary cycles, stars and sacred time.

For those who look at the sky and feel the cosmos speaking. You learn the essentials of esoteric astrology, planetary cycles and sacred timings in a simple, approachable way.

Spell & Sigil Witch Path

Practical magic, candles, jars and words of power.

For those who feel called to make magic in a practical, hands-on way. You learn the basics of crafting spells, working with candles, jars, sigils and formulas with responsibility and awareness.

All teachings on herbs, crystals and rituals are offered exclusively for spiritual and esoteric purposes. They do not constitute medical advice in any way and do not replace healthcare or psychotherapeutic support.
