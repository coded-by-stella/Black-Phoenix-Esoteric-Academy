How you study: flexible, structured and fully guided.

One lesson per week, with sacred pauses

All journeys follow the recommended rhythm of one lesson per week during active periods, so you can integrate without rushing and stay grounded in everyday life.

Each Pillar is made of 6 connected lessons. Each full Path includes 7 Pillars, for a total of 42 lessons.

Live lessons, recordings or both

Lessons can be live, pre-recorded or live with replay available. The weekly lesson is always guaranteed during active calendar periods, even if the teacher is travelling or busy.

PDF materials (only when they truly help)

Some lessons include PDF handouts with theory, rituals and further study. Others focus only on guided practice, intuitive exercises or personal notes.

Materials are not guaranteed for every single lesson; they are offered when the topic really benefits from tables, diagrams or supporting texts.

Quizzes, exams and resits

Along the way you will meet short self-assessment quizzes to see what you have integrated and which parts you want to review.

Each Pillar, each Path of study and the complete 5-Path curriculum have a final exam. If you do not pass an exam you can retake it once for free. From the third attempt onwards there is a ‚Ç¨ 27 fee for each new session.


Study levels at the Academy

1. Single class

A Single class is one standalone lesson from the official curriculum. It focuses on a specific topic, such as a ritual, a technique, a practical aspect or devotional work, and includes the lesson, possible PDFs and a short final quiz.

At the end you receive a Black Phoenix Esoteric Academy Certificate of Attendance for that single class. This is not a professional diploma.

You can choose to attend only a few classes or, over time, complete all the classes that form a Pillar or a Path: single lessons add up and later allow you to take the exam and obtain the corresponding certificate.

2. Pillar (6 lessons)

A Pillar is a block of 6 connected lessons on a specific sub-topic (for example protection, esoteric botanicals, rituals, shadow work, lunar work, elements‚Ä¶).

At the end of the Pillar, after the required quizzes, you receive a Black Phoenix Esoteric Academy Pillar Completion Certificate.

3. Path of study (7 Pillars ¬∑ 42 lessons)

A Path of study is a structured curriculum made of 7 Pillars, for a total of 42 lessons fully dedicated to a specific stream of practice.

All students begin with the Path of the Foundations of Occult Esoteric Art, then they can choose one or more specialization Paths.

At the end of each Path, after the final exam, you receive a Path Completion Certificate. Some Paths give access to IAOTH certifications, as explained below.

4. Complete 5-Path journey

The Complete journey includes the Foundations Path and all 4 specialization Paths.

This is the professional route for those who feel witchcraft and the esoteric arts as a life vocation and want to reach the international Diploma ‚ÄúHolistic Esoteric Operator (IAOTH Accredited)‚Äù.

Certificates, IAOTH Certifications and final Diploma

Internal Black Phoenix certificates

Every Single class, every Pillar and every Path of study awards an official Black Phoenix Esoteric Academy Certificate of Attendance or Completion, clearly stating what you have studied.

IAOTH Certifications ‚Äì Specialization Paths

By completing the Path of the Foundations of Occult Esoteric Art and one of the specialization Paths (Green Magic, Lunar Magic, Elemental Magic, Eclectic Witch), and passing the final exam, you can obtain the corresponding IAOTH certificate as:

  • Green Witchcraft Practitioner (Path of Green Magic)
  • Lunar Witchcraft Practitioner (Path of Lunar Magic)

Lessons can be attended either as full Paths or by slowly accumulating single classes: once you have completed all lessons required for a Path and the Foundations Path, you can take the exam and request the corresponding IAOTH certificate.

Completing the Foundations Path and all 4 main Paths, and successfully passing all required exams, allows you to obtain the international Diploma ‚ÄúHolistic Esoteric Operator (IAOTH Accredited)‚Äù.

The Diploma allows you to work professionally in the holistic and esoteric field, according to your country‚Äôs regulations, and to teach the practices you have learned. The IAOTH recognition applies only to specialization Paths completed with exam and to the complete 5-Path curriculum with final exam passed.
