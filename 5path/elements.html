What is the Elemental Magic Path?

The Elemental Magic Path is for witches who want to understand and live Earth, Air, Fire, Water and Spirit as sacred and intelligent forces, present in the body, in nature, in ritual and in everyday life.

Through the 7 Pillars you will explore esoteric theory of the Elements, correspondences, ritual work, elemental altars, practices for balance, protection and transformation.

The recommended pace is one lesson per week, giving you time to embody each Element inside yourself, without rushing, in tune with your inner rhythm.
