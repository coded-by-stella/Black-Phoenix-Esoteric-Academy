What is the Green Witchcraft Path?

The Green Witchcraft Path is the path of witches who learn directly from plants, forests, fields, rivers and wild places. It is a practice rooted in animism, folk magic and a deep relationship with the land.

Through 7 Pillars you will explore herbal correspondences, spiritual uses of plants, offerings, seasonal rites, protection, prosperity, ancestral rural wisdom and the art of bringing nature magic into everyday life.

The Path is designed with a rhythm of one lesson per week, so that you can integrate, practice and keep up with your ordinary life without rushing.
