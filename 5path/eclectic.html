What is the Path of the Eclectic Witch?

The Path of the Eclectic Witch is for those who feel they do not belong to a single current, but can weave together different traditions: folk magic, wicca, lunar work, elemental magic, northern and Mediterranean currents, shamanic and intuitive practices.

It is not an invitation to ‚Äúpick randomly‚Äù, but a journey to create a structured personal Art: with clear criteria, respect for sources, ethics, inner coherence and responsibility toward the invisible world.

The recommended pace is one lesson per week, giving you time to experiment, reflect, integrate and let every passage settle in your life.

Each Pillar is a coherent block of 6 lessons. Together they guide you to become a conscious Eclectic Witch, with a clear, grounded practice.

  • Ritual of commitment to honor sources and spirits

PILLAR 4 ‚Äì Personal altar and pantheon
