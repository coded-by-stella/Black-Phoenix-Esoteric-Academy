What is the Foundations Path?

The Path of the Foundations of Occult Esoteric Art is the first step for every Black Phoenix witch. Before complex rituals, specializations and titles, here you learn to stand in your magic in a safe, centered and responsible way.

It is a Path that works on protection, altar, tools, energy, sacred space and the structure of your practice. It helps you move from the idea of ‚Äúdoing some rituals‚Äù to the construction of a truly personal, coherent and well-anchored Art.

The journey is designed with a recommended pace of one lesson per week, so you have time to integrate, experiment and observe how your practice changes in everyday life.
